Physical Atlas / Delivery Topology
01 / Two Distribution Paths

Path A: Internal (Not Recommended)

Placing models inside Content/ for Pak packaging.

  • Results in massive patch sizes.
  • Some virtualized file systems may interfere with mmap efficiency.

Path B: External (Recommended)

Storing models in Saved/Models/ or custom external paths.

  • Supports independent model downloaders; lightweight executable.
  • Physics Edge: 100% compatible with OS native mmap, lowest latency.
